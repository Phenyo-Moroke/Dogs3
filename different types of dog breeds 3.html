Different types of breeds

3.Non-Sporting Dogs
  • Breeds that do not possess qualities that make it fit for inclusion in one of these other groups.
  • Popular breeds such as the Poodle, Dalmatian, and the Bulldog.
    • 4.Terrier Dogs
    • Terrier breeds were created to go after rodents and other small vermin,as well as hunting small animals including rats, otters, and badgers.
    • short-legged terriers were bred to pursuit rodents underground, while terriers with long legs are able to dig out these creatures rather than burrowing in after them.
    • Common traits are of self-confidence and courage, as they are determined to do what it takes to locate its quarry no matter the terrain
    • Other traits incude being energetic and feisty as well as having stubborn personalities.
    • Popular breeds include Bull Terriers, Scottish Terriers, and West Highland White Terriers.
      • 5.Hound Dogs
      • Include dogs that have specifically been bred to pursue warm-blooded quarry.
      • Sighthounds have sleek, long legs that they rely on to have their explosive speed, incredible stamina, and sharp, wide vision to chase fast animals like jackrabbits and antelope.
      • Scenthounds had to track down their prey, which sometimes even included convicts who had escaped from prison.
      • Popular breeds Bloodhounds, Dachshunds, and Greyhounds.
