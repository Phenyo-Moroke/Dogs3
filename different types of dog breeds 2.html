Different types of breeds

2.Sporting Dogs
  • Dogs that are used to aid hunters in retrieving game birds.
  • These dogs are often great swimmers, able to retrieve waterfowl from the water .
  • May have thick coats to protect them from brush and brambles when making their way through thick forestation.
  • Popular sporting breeds include Labrador Retriever,German Shorthaired Pointer,English Springer Spaniel.
  • There are four basic types of Sporting dogs:spaniels, pointers, retrievers, and setters.
  • Some of the breeds in this group, such as retrievers, are especially adept at swimming and specialize in waterfowl like ducks, while setters, spaniels, and pointing breeds are known experts in hunting quail, pheasant, and other game birds in the grasslands.
